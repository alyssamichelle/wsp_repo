Local Chinchilla Breeders

Quite often I do not have rescues up for adoption. A very knowledgable and local breeder is Lyn Shuster of Lyn's Chins. She has kits and youngster chinchillas that are also looking for their forever home! It is always better to purchase from a local breeder than a petstore. With a petstore chin you get no background information or even a choice in color! Check out her website.

Surrender

Here at Orlando Chinchilla Rescue, we understand that things change. Circumstances may not allow you to keep your chin. We take in any chinchillas (or small animals) in the central Florida area. Under certain circumstances, we will be willing to drive a distance to pick up a surrender in other parts of Florida.

We do not charge anyone to surrender animals, we also do not pay for animals that are going to be surrendered.

    We strive to find the best possible homes for all of our chins. If you need to rehome your chinchilla please email me.

    When turning over a chinchilla (or other small animal), all supplies (food,hay , dust , toys etc) and the cage are needed. This is to ensure that the chin can go from one enviroment to another with as little stress as possible.
